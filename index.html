About Me
~& █

Hello! I'm Liam, a 15-year-old student living in Austin, TX. I'm interested in all things computer science, from backend development to GLSL shaders. Currently, I'm pursuing my interest in environmental data science, and plan to attend a data science program this summer at TCU. Outside of programming, I enjoy playing the piano, and have done so for the last 11 years. I also joined a competitive rock climbing team a few years ago, and since then it has become my main sport in addition to something I do for fun. This summer, I will spend a month in Norway, where I will not only discover a foreign culture, but also go lead climbing with several other kids my age. Finally comes my interest in mathematics. I've always found math to be interested, and it has since inspired projects and course selections, and is likely one of the reasons data science is one of my main interests.

- Liam 

I've been playing piano ever since I was 4 (almost 12 years now!). In middle school, I participated in my school's band in addition to private lessons. In high school, also in addition to private lessons, I played in my school's jazz band, and plan to join orchestra next (junior) year. The orchestra director has asked me to play Rachmaninoff's second piano concerto (the second movement), and it's a piece I've wanted to play for a long time.
